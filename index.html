Who We Are

The Makers of Entertaining Games Association (MEGA) is a student run organization which strives to promote and facilitate the art of game creation within the University of Southern California.

We organize game jams and student speaker events to build a community of game developers of all experience levels and strengths.

Our purpose is to create a platform for student game developers to network, collaborate and learn from each other through peer-based events and game jams, as well as provide resources to help grow their interests and careers.

NEWBIES & VETS GAME JAM 2022

The wait is over, and its time once more for the annual MEGA Newbies and Vets Game Jam!

Have you never made a game before but are dying to try it? Are you looking to meet people and collaborate on a small portfolio piece? Just looking to have some fun over the weekend? Well you're in luck!

The MEGA Newbies and Vets game jam pairs up groups of veterans and rookies from across USC to spend a weekend making a small game based on a yet to be revealed theme

Join us this Friday for an exciting weekend of non-stop game development action! We especially welcome newbies with little to no game development experience! Interested? Use the QR code to sign up or click here! (Signups end Wednesday at 11:59 PM)

Opening ceremony is Friday, October 21st at 5PM in SCI 108! You don't want to miss it!
